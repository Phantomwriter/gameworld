Thank you for trying Gameworld. When you sign up, the world of games is at your finger tips

Deals and Question of the Week

$tuff your head with knowledge about all things games. There'll be a new question every day and of course links that take you to places where you can get coupons and deals on all your favorite games.

Console Games

Check out the latest news, information and any deals we can find on your favorite game systems. We'll make sure to keep them up to date because we know times are tough, but that shouldn't stop you from playing your favorite games!

On-line Games

We'll have updated links from websites where you can play free on-line games for casual play. Places like pogo.com and addictinggames.com. or, pay and play sites like WOW and many more!

Mobile Apps

In this section, we'll have articles and links on the latest popular mobile app releases. Didn't you wish you knew about Angry Birds and Fruit ninja when they first came out?

Card Games

This is the house favorite! Do you play Magic The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh or Pokemon? Here's where you can get links to the latest news like new releases, popular cards, deck ideas and deck strategies. We're working on a deck database where you can construct and save your favorite deck types. For you regular card players, check in for ideas on fun games like Kings in the Corner, Rumy and poker!

What year was the first Playstation introduced?

In 1994, the new PlayStation X (PSX) was released.
It was no longer compatable with Nintendo game cartridges and only played CD-ROM based games.
Playstation soon became the best selling game console.
